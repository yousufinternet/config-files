Title: Senegal opens new art museum honoring black civilization
Author: 
Date: Fri, 07 Dec 2018 18:07:51 +0300
Link: http://feeds.reuters.com/~r/news/artsculture/~3/t2GBTxIbB7o/senegal-opens-new-art-museum-honoring-black-civilization-idUSKBN1O61JT
 
Senegal has opened one of Africa's largest art collections, one of a series of 
new museums on the continent that could eventually receive thousands of 
artefacts from European museums that were looted during the colonial era.[image
